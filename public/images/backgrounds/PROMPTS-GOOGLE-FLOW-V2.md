# Prompts Google Flow (Veo 2) — Animation des 9 images Nano Banana
# SmartSchool SN — Image to Video

Importe chaque image dans Google Flow, active le mode "Image to Video",
puis colle le prompt correspondant. Durée recommandée : 8-10 secondes.

## IMAGE 1 — bg-role-classroom.png
### Salle de classe / Côté gauche Role-Selector

```
Cinematic slow push-in shot moving gently forward through the classroom.
The teacher's hand moves smoothly across the glowing green whiteboard,
writing equations that leave neon green (#00E676) light trails.
Students subtly shift in their seats, heads turning with curiosity.
Golden god rays from the windows intensify slowly, dust particles
float and sparkle in the beams. The neon green whiteboard glow
pulses softly like a heartbeat. Depth of field breathing slowly.
Camera movement: very slow dolly forward, 24fps, cinematic.
Mood: warm, academic, alive.
```

## PARAMETRES GOOGLE FLOW RECOMMANDES

| Paramètre | Valeur |
|-----------|--------|
| **Mode** | Image to Video |
| **Durée** | 8 secondes |
| **Aspect ratio** | 16:9 (widescreen) |
| **Motion intensity** | Low à Medium (mouvements subtils) |
| **Camera motion** | Selon chaque prompt |
| **Quality** | High / Cinematic |
